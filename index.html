About

Laura Shummon Maass

I grew up in Milwaukee Wisconsin, but have lived in over 8 different cities, each with their own unique voice and personality. I would describe my photography style as a mix of street and landscape, but I have a strong desire to move more in the direction of documentary photography. Photography to me is about capturing viewer interest, and telling the story of a person or place.

My favorite places where I've lived

Dubrovnik, Croatia

A Uenesco hertiage site found right along the coast of adriatic sea

Address:

Tvrđava Minčeta, Dubrovačke Gradske Zidine, Ul. Ispod Minčete 9, 20000, Dubrovnik, Croatia

Why I liked living here

Dubrovnik is a distinctive Old Town, encircled with massive stone walls. It feels like you are stepping into history when you wander through the streets of this old town. If you liked the HBO series Game of Thrones you will have plenty of tours to do here as this was one of the main filming locations for the series. To fans it is best known as the home of the Lancaster family.
